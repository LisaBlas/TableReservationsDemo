🍽 Restaurants

Table Reservations

Accept online reservations without paying OpenTable 15% commissions. Own your customer relationships and data—no middleman required.

Visual floor plan

See all tables and reservations at a glance. Timeline bars show when each table is occupied—no more double-bookings.

Smart auto-assignment

System automatically picks the best-fit table for party size. Or manually assign to specific tables when needed.

Guest notes & pre-orders

Track dietary restrictions, special occasions, or pre-ordered dishes. Everything you need to know before guests arrive.

Day-by-day navigation

Switch between dates instantly. Each day's reservations are stored separately—plan weeks ahead or review past bookings.

Conflict detection

System warns if you try to book an occupied table. Prevents double-bookings and scheduling mistakes automatically.

Why this works: OpenTable charges €2-3 per cover plus monthly fees. For busy restaurants, that's thousands per year. This system costs €25/month total. No commissions, no lock-in, no gatekeepers.

Quick start: Add a reservation with guest name and party size. The system auto-assigns the best table. Try navigating between dates to see how reservations persist.
